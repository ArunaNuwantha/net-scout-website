Privacy Policy for Net Scout

Effective Date: April 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Net Scout. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Information

Net Scout requires access to your device's location to scan for WiFi networks. This is a technical requirement of the Android operating system, as scanning for WiFi networks requires location permissions.

WiFi Network Information

We collect information about WiFi networks in your vicinity, including:

  • SSID (network name)
  • BSSID (network identifier)
  • Signal strength
  • Security type
  • Channel information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of:

  • Displaying WiFi network information to you within the app
  • Analyzing signal strength and network characteristics
  • Providing network scanning functionality

Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local Storage Only: All data collected is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your information with any third parties.

Permissions

Net Scout requires the following permissions:

  • Location: Required to scan for WiFi networks
  • WiFi Access: Required to access WiFi functionality
  • Internet: Required for app functionality

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@netscout.app
